WELCOME TO KYARUSOZI HEALTH CENTER IV


Welcome to Kyarusozi Health Center IV, a government-aided health facility located in Kyenjojo District, Kyarusozi Town Council. We are dedicated to providing quality healthcare service delivery to all people within the surrounding community with equality and fairness from all backgrounds.

Our health center, previously operating as a health center II and later upgraded to a health center III, has now been further promoted to a health center IV due to the continuously increasing demand for health care services and community Health Needs Assessment.
